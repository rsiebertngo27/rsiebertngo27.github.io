Joint Health: Carpal Tunnel Syndrome and Cervical Radiculopathy

Why Care About Joint Health?

The obvious reason is without your joints working properly you wouldn't be able to move. However on a more serious note, if you begin taking care of your joints at a younger age then it will lead to a happier and healthier life when you get older, and can minimize the risk for arthritis and other age related joint ailments.
